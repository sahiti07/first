All about choices and chances:-

Two roads diverged in a wood, and I took the one less travelled by, and that has made all the differences. This is an excerpt from the poem,"The Road Not Taken" by a great poet of all times - Robert Frost.

It speaks about different choices we have, different decisions we take, different calculations we consider, different results we get. But it all starts at a starting point where the diversion starts. At this time,when the 21 st century is no more new, our young learners have exposure to a wide range of subjects.

Advancement in our technology has certainly contributed to these choices.Gone are the days when 9 out of 10 students had the same answers for the most popular(and obvious)question we come across child hood -'What do you want to become when you grow up?'.The trend of children want to become engineers or doctors slowly started dimnishing by the end of the 20thcentury.Even arts like painting,fine arts,dance starting to find a great place in the proffesional world.Games & sports,which we are finding to be 'aparthied'slowly started gaining momentum and celebrities started emerging from them.
