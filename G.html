Republic Day

India was under the British rule for many years.During this period,Indians were forced to follow the British laws. The Indian freedom fighters struggled for a long period and finally India became independent on the 15 th of August ,1947. Two and half years after this, the Indian Government implemented its own Constitution and declared India as a Democratic Republic on the 26 th of January ,1950.People ofIndia started celebrating 26 th of January as Republic Day every year.

On this day,every Indian should sincerely promise to safeguard the Constitution,maintain peace and harmony as well as support in the development of our country..

It is indeed a great honor for Indians to celebrate Republic Day. It is a great importance and is celebrated by people with joy and enthusiasm. Various events are organized on this day. A big celebration is held in the national capital,New Delhi. It is also celebrated in the state capitals all over India.The celebration starts with the unfolding of the National flag by the President of India and singing the National Anthem. Following this,Indian army does a lot of programs.
